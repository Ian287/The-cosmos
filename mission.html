ISS

The international space station is a collaborative orbitting research laboratory in low earth orbit , jointly operated by five space agencies NASA ,JAXA,ESA,CSA and Roscosmos. It has been active since late 2000 bringing together international flight crews and multiple launch vehicles. It provides insight in training ,engineering and developmant in zero gravity environmrnt. It orbits earth at an altitude of 400km circling the earth every 93 minutes averaging 15 orbits in a day . although the purpose of the ISS was to act as a lab , observatory and factory while providing for transportation , later it was given adittional roles such as serving commercial, diplomatic and educational purposes . The ISS surely stands as a wonder of the modern word .

Europa Clipper

This is a space probe developed by NASA to study Europa , a moon of jupiter. Launched in october 14 2024 .the main aim is to explore Europa for habitability and aid in selection of a landing site for the proposed europa lander .The spacecraft carries scientific instruments which will be used to analyze the potential presence of geothermal activity and the moon's induced magnetic field . it used the gravity assist from Mars on march 1 2025 and will later use a gravity assist from Earth in december 2026 before arriving at Europa on april 2030
